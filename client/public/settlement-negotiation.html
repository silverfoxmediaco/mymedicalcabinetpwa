Frequently Asked Questions

How do I send a settlement offer to a medical biller?

After our AI analyzes your bill and recommends a fair offer amount, you can send a settlement offer directly to the biller with one click. The offer is delivered via secure email with OTP verification, and you can track the status in real-time.

What happens after a biller accepts my settlement offer?

When a biller accepts your offer, you'll receive a notification and can pay the accepted amount instantly through our secure Stripe payment integration. Once payment is confirmed, both you and the biller receive a settlement confirmation.

Can the biller counter my settlement offer?

Yes. Billers can accept, decline, or counter your offer. If they counter with a different amount, you'll see the counter offer in your dashboard and can choose to accept it, decline it, or submit a new offer. The full negotiation history is tracked.

How long do settlement offers remain valid?

Settlement offers remain active for 7 days. If the biller doesn't respond within that time, the offer automatically expires and you can submit a new one if you choose. You'll be notified of all status changes.
