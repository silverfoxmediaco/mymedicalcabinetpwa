Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI medical bill analysis work?

Simply upload a photo or PDF of your medical bill. Our AI reads the bill, identifies the procedure codes and charges, then compares them against fair market pricing databases to determine if you're being overcharged and recommends a fair settlement amount.

Can AI really find errors on my medical bill?

Yes. Studies show that up to 80% of medical bills contain errors. Our AI checks for duplicate charges, incorrect procedure codes, unbundling errors, and charges for services not rendered. Catching even one error can save you hundreds or thousands of dollars.

Is my medical bill information kept private?

Absolutely. Your bill data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell your information to third parties. Only you can access your uploaded bills and AI analysis results through your secure account.

What types of medical bills can I analyze?

You can analyze any medical bill including hospital bills, physician charges, lab work, imaging, emergency room visits, surgical procedures, and outpatient services. Simply upload a clear photo or PDF of the bill.
