The Medical Debt Opportunity: 10% Bill Pay Revenue

The Collection Crisis

$220B+ in medical debt held by US consumers the #1 source of debt in collections
$68B in hospital bad debt written off annually revenue that providers never collect
57% of patients delay or avoid care due to cost uncertainty lost revenue for providers
$0.15 is all collection agencies typically recover per $1 of medical debt an 85% loss rate

How MMC Solves This

1 Patient uploads bill OCR extracts line items, charges, and provider details automatically
2 AI analyzes fair pricing compares charges against real CMS Medicare rates and regional benchmarks to calculate a fair offer
3 Patient sends settlement offer one-tap offer sent directly to the biller with a clear, professional proposal
4 Biller accepts, Stripe processes payment instant, guaranteed payment. MMC earns 10% of every transaction

The Psychological Advantage: Why Billers Accept

✅
Guaranteed Cash Now

A partial payment today beats a full balance that sits in AR for 180+ days. Most billers know that aging debt rapidly loses value 60% of bills over 90 days are never collected.

📈
Data-Backed Fair Offer

The AI-generated offer isn't arbitrary it's anchored to Medicare rates and regional averages. Billers see a credible, justified number, not a lowball. This shifts the negotiation from emotional to rational.

💰
Zero Collection Cost

Collection agencies take 25-50% of recovered amounts and damage patient relationships. MMC offers a frictionless, dignified path biller gets more money with zero collection overhead.

Revenue Model Example

Patient receives a $3,200 hospital bill → AI recommends a fair offer of $1,850 → Biller accepts (getting guaranteed payment vs. potential write-off) → Patient saves $1,350 → MMC earns $185 (10%) on the transaction

At scale: 10,000 settlements/month × $185 avg fee = $1.85M/month recurring
