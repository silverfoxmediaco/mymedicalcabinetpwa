Frequently Asked Questions

How do I share my medical records with a doctor?

Select which records you want to share (medications, conditions, allergies, doctors, etc.), enter the recipient's email address, and send. The doctor receives a secure link and must verify with a one-time passcode before viewing your records. You can also generate a QR code at your appointment for instant access.

Is sharing my medical records through MyMedicalCabinet secure?

Yes. All shared records are protected with OTP (one-time passcode) verification. The recipient must enter a code sent to their email before they can view any records. Shared links are time-limited and you can revoke access at any time.

Can I choose which records to share?

Absolutely. You have full control over what you share. Select specific record types like medications, conditions, allergies, surgical history, immunizations, doctors, and insurance information. You can share everything or just the records relevant to your visit.

What is the Patient Intake Form feature?

The Patient Intake Form is a shareable, read-only summary of your complete medical profile. Instead of filling out paperwork at every new doctor visit, share your intake form link and let the office view your medications, conditions, allergies, and more — all pre-filled from your MyMedicalCabinet account.
